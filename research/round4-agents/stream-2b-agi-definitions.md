**Research complete.** Here is the summary of what was done and the key findings:

## What Was Done

Seven research domains were investigated across primary sources (Chollet's full paper via HTML extraction, Harnad's original paper, Stanford SEP entries on Chinese Room and Embodied Cognition), secondary literature (Legg-Hutter formal definition, Marcus & Davis book reviews, AI history analysis papers), and current empirical results (ARC benchmark data through 2025, neuro-symbolic AI surveys).

## Key Findings

**Where AMA is genuinely strong:**
- It is the hybrid symbolic-neural architecture Marcus & Davis explicitly advocate. This is a real architectural advance.
- Experiential co-occurrence grounding is richer than arbitrary symbol manipulation, making a partial answer to Searle and Harnad.
- The metacognitive Mirror layer addresses what Chollet identifies as meta-learning priors — potentially the most important variable in his framework.
- On Clark's extended mind thesis, the Matrix *is* a cognitive constituent, not a mere tool.
- AMA avoids the knowledge acquisition bottleneck and pure-statistical brittleness that killed expert systems and pure connectionism.

**Where AMA is definitively incomplete:**
1. **Causal reasoning is missing.** PMI/Hebbian = Pearl's Layer 1 (association). Intervention and counterfactual reasoning are absent. This is the single most important gap, and it is technically addressable.
2. **LLM entanglement is unmeasured.** The claim that "the graph is the mind" cannot be verified without empirical isolation of the graph's contribution vs. the LLM's pre-trained weights.
3. **Embodied grounding is absent.** The agent has never been cold, hungry, or in danger. Whether this is *necessary* for general intelligence is contested, but the strongest philosophical arguments (Harnad, Varela) say it matters.
4. **The Mirror is not consciousness — it is sophisticated metacognition.** Calling it consciousness is naming inflation that follows a recurring historical pattern.
5. **Developer-unaware generalization has not been demonstrated.** This is Chollet's actual test, and it has not been attempted.

**The historical pattern warning:**
Every AI era had genuine capability gains, a compelling architectural story, and a philosophical framing that made it look like the path to AGI. Each hit a wall. AMA has genuine gains and a compelling architecture. The wall has not yet been found. That is reason for disciplined testing, not for concluding the wall does not exist.
